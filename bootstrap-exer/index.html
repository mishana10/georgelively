Meet George Lively, a name that resonates with triumph in the world of horse racing. With a natural flair for nurturing equine potential, George has become a distinguished figure in the realm of training. His journey is punctuated by remarkable horses that have not only left their hoofprints on the tracks but have also etched their stories into the hearts of racing enthusiasts.

One such luminary is Persistence, a mare whose grace and determination led her to victory in the prestigious G1 Nassau Stakes. Under George's meticulous guidance, Persistence galloped her way into the history books, showcasing the trainer's adeptness at unlocking a horse's true potential.

In the stables, the promise of the future is embodied by Josseline, an exciting two-year-old filly. George's discerning eye has identified her as a rising star, brimming with potential to capture hearts and trophies alike in the seasons to come.

The diversity of George's training prowess is exemplified by Boy Ho Ho, a versatile five-year-old handicapper. Boy Ho Ho's prowess spans both flat and hurdle races, a testament to George's skill in honing horses for a range of challenges.

Staying Alive, a six-year-old gelding, stands as a testament to George's ability to nurture latent talents. This gelding's impressive performance over hurdles speaks volumes about George's capacity to refine skills and transform potential into excellence.

Among the stable's veterans shines Finner, a nine-year-old gelding with an awe-inspiring record. Having triumphed in eight out of twelve starts over fences, Finner embodies George's mastery in maintaining a horse's competitive edge and spirit year after year.

In the hands of George Lively, these horses emerge not just as racers, but as living testaments to his dedication, expertise, and passion. Each stride they take on the track is a reflection of the bond between trainer and athlete, a bond that drives them to achieve nothing short of greatness.
